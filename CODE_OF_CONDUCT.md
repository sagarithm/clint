# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume positive intent.
3. Provide actionable feedback.
4. Focus on ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, abuse, or discrimination.
2. Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
3. Sharing private information without consent.
4. Any behavior that makes participation unsafe.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project maintainers through a private issue or direct contact.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, or restrict participation for violations.
